The Structures tab gives you original, ready-to-run cooperative routines. This page maps the wider world of teaching methods and frameworks teachers ask about — thinking routines, questioning techniques, discussion protocols, and named systems. We don't reproduce copyrighted material; each entry says where it comes from, links to the source, and carries a badge telling you what you're allowed to do with it.
